double getTotalFreeSpace()

Get the storage services total free space (incurs simulated overhead)

Synchronously asks the storage service for its total free space capacity, that is, the total capacity at the / path. Note that this doesnt mean that that free space could be used to store a single file, as the storage service may have file systems at multiple mount points, may me a front-end for a set of storage systems, etc.

Service free space method

Returns:
